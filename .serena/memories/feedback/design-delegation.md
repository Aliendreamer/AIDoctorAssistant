# Design delegation

**Type:** feedback (how to work with this user)

On UI/frontend work the user said "use your frontend skill for the design i am not good at design" and "give me sample" when asked to pick a color.

**Why:** They don't want to choose between abstract design options (hex codes, typography terms). They want a design lead to make opinionated calls and show something concrete to react to.

**How to apply:** Make the design decisions yourself (grounded in the frontend-design skill), then produce a real, clickable/visual sample — an HTML mockup published as an Artifact, or a browser screenshot of the actual running app — and let them react to that. Reserve questions for genuine either/or choices (e.g. accent color) framed with a visual, and lead with a recommendation. The one place they engaged well was picking an accent color from a live mockup with a switcher.
